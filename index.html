Common Problems & Solutions

CSP Bypass Issue

This often happens when your game or server requires a specific version of Custom Shaders Patch. To fix it, open Content Manager, go to the 'Custom Shaders Patch' settings, and install the latest recommended version. This will resolve most bypass errors and let you join the server.
